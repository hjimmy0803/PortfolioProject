About Me

My name is Jimmy Hernandez and I am excited to call myself a developer. I did not start of this way as my path towards beimg a developer took many turns but proud to be here.

During my adolescent years I was busy playing the trumpet, involved in multiple sports, and enjoyed my time being involved with the Boy Scout of America. After high school my mimd was set to join the military and became a Military Police officer in the Army National Guard for 8 years. I was proud to serve my country and wanted to carry that over in my civilian life so I became a motivated Border Patrol Agent in San Diego, California for 9 years.

Since my time in Border Patrol my personal life grew as I got married and shortly after had three kids. I felt the need for change, since I was in a dangerous profession and seeked out my destiny. I back to school and was certified as an hvac technician. Shortly after I taught myself to effeceintly read blueprints and was to catch mistakes and solve issues that engineers and architects were making. This where I had an epiphany and thought maybe I can be a great developer as well and see what I can bring to the table and well and take care of own growing family.
